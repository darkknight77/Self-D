DISHEVELED
##########
A person who has his hair uncombed, his shirt untucked and stains on his shirt is an example of someone who would be described as disheveled.


1. His bleached hair was disheveled, his eyes squinting at the hall light.

2. His eyes were red, her red hair wet and disheveled, and she wore a flannel bathrobe and was barefoot.

3. The youth was tall, erect, and had disheveled blond hair.

BAFFLING
########
impossible to understand; perplexing

1. He walked through the halls quickly, the emerging thoughts in his head baffling him.

2. The complex puzzle baffled me for several hours before i could connect all the pieces.

3.The woman's confusing testimony left the jury quite baffled.

4. As a serious write, I am baffled by the success of silly novels about teenagers.
